ABOUT US

A website lending a helping hand for Alzheimers patients and their caretakers.We provide sequential medical checkups,daily
yoga sessions with expert trainers,memory exercises,mind relaxing activities and always try to keep the patients active and
engaged.

In addition,we ensure complete guidance to the caretakers through sessions followed by experienced doctors.At the end of
the day we will provide up-to -date medical reports with progress charts.
